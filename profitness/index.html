About Company
British & Singapore Army Training Center

Welcome to Gurkha Pro-Fitness, a premier facility for training British and Singapore army candidates. Our center offers a comprehensive program that includes both physical and mental training, as well as educational classes. Our state-of-the-art facilities include a dedicated training ground and a modern hostel to accommodate all of our candidates. Our experienced instructors are committed to helping each and every candidate reach their full potential and achieve success in their military careers.
